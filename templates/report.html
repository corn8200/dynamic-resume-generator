Weekend Trip Report: Shenandoah Overlook

Trip Date: July 27–28, 2025 • Reported by John Cornelius

Overview

Quick two-day hike with the family exploring Blue Ridge vistas, local wildlife, and backcountry trails. Logged trail times, gear tests, and some wildlife observations.

Highlights

  • Trailhead: Raven Rock, Entry 103B
  • Total Distance: 6.4 miles
  • Spotted: Black bear cub, 2 deer, red fox
  • New boots passed comfort/waterproof test

Notes & Lessons

Don’t trust the NOAA forecast under the canopy — storm moved in early. Gear deployment drills went well. Need to refine morning packing procedure for better speed.

Pro Tip: Store firestarter in backup Ziplock inside gaiter pouch — not inside the main med kit.
